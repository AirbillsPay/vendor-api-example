Airbills is a vendor gateway API that lets your application pay Nigerian utility bills — airtime, electricity, internet data, cable TV, betting wallet top-ups, and transport — on behalf of your users. Every payment is settled in USDT or USDC on the Solana blockchain, so you get fast, low-cost settlement without touching traditional payment rails. Integrate once and give your users seamless access to the bills they pay every day.

## Get up and running

Follow these steps to go from zero to your first successful bill payment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your secret key">
    Sign in to the vendor dashboard at [business.airbills.org](https://business.airbills.org/) and generate your vendor secret key. You'll attach this key to every API request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your environment">
    Add your secret key and the API base URL to your environment variables so they're never hard-coded in your source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate a transaction with POST /transact">
    Call `POST /transact` with your chosen product code, payment mode, and the bill details. The API returns a transaction ID and — for `default` mode — a Solana transaction instruction ready to sign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the payment with POST /transact/process">
    Call `POST /transact/process` with the transaction ID to finalise the bill payment. A `status` of `"00"` in the response confirms success.
  </Step>
</Steps>
