Every successful transaction you process through the Airbills Business API earns you a commission on the transaction amount. Commissions are credited automatically — no manual claims required.

## Commission Rates

### Airtime

| Network | Commission |
|---------|-----------|
| MTN | 1% |
| Airtel | 1% |
| GLO | 2% |
| T2Mobile | 2% |

### Internet Data

| Network | Commission |
|---------|-----------|
| MTN | 1% |
| Airtel | 1% |
| GLO | 2% |
| T2Mobile | 2% |

### Electricity

| Category | Commission |
|----------|-----------|
| All providers | 0.2% |

### Cable TV

| Category | Commission |
|----------|-----------|
| All providers | 0.5% |

## How It Works

Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the transaction amount and credited to your business wallet in **USDC** after each successful transaction.

For example, if a customer tops up ₦10,000 of MTN airtime through your integration, you earn 1% of the equivalent USDC value.
